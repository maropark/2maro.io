An Obsidian community plugin that adds an "Open terminal here" command to the palette and file-explorer right-click menu. Detects the platform automatically and lets you pick from 15+ pre-configured terminal apps (Warp, iTerm2, kitty, GNOME Terminal, Windows Terminal…) or supply a custom `{dir}` command template.
